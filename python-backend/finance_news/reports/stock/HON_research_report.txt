# Financial News Report on Honeywell International Inc. (HON) Stock - September 16, 2024

## Introduction

Honeywell International Inc. (NASDAQ: HON) is a diversified technology and manufacturing company that operates in various sectors, including aerospace, building technologies, performance materials, and safety and productivity solutions. As of September 16, 2024, the stock has been the subject of significant attention due to various key events and financial updates. This report aims to provide a comprehensive analysis of the current state of HON stock, including recent news, financial performance, analyst forecasts, and key events that have influenced its market performance.

## Recent Financial Performance

### Quarterly Earnings and Revenue

Honeywell International Inc. reported its Q2 2024 earnings on July 25, 2024. The company posted earnings per share (EPS) of $2.49, surpassing the consensus estimate of $2.42 by $0.07. Revenue for the quarter increased by 4.7% compared to the same period last year ([MarketBeat](https://www.marketbeat.com/stocks/NASDAQ/HON/)). This positive earnings surprise indicates strong operational performance and effective cost management.

### Annual Financial Performance

In 2023, Honeywell's revenue was $36.66 billion, marking a 3.37% increase from the previous year's $35.47 billion. Earnings for the year were $5.66 billion, reflecting a 13.93% increase ([Stock Analysis](https://stockanalysis.com/stocks/hon/)). These figures highlight the company's robust financial health and its ability to generate consistent revenue and profit growth.

## Analyst Ratings and Forecasts

### Analyst Ratings

According to eight analysts, the average rating for HON stock is "Buy." The 12-month stock price forecast is $223.13, which represents a 9.30% increase from the latest price of $204.15 ([Stock Analysis](https://stockanalysis.com/stocks/hon/forecast/)). This bullish sentiment is supported by the company's strong financial performance and strategic initiatives.

### Price Targets

The analysts' price targets for HON stock range from a low estimate of $210 to a high estimate of $244. The average target of $223.13 suggests a positive outlook for the stock over the next year ([Stock Analysis](https://stockanalysis.com/stocks/hon/forecast/)). This range indicates that while there is some variability in expectations, the overall sentiment remains optimistic.

## Key Events and Developments

### Morgan Stanley's 12th Annual Laguna Conference

On September 11, 2024, Honeywell's Chairman and CEO, Vimal Kapur, participated in Morgan Stanley's 12th Annual Laguna Conference. During the conference, Kapur emphasized that the growth of Honeywell's aerospace business next year would be determined by its ability to supply parts to Boeing and Airbus, rather than demand ([Stock Analysis](https://stockanalysis.com/stocks/hon/)). This statement underscores the importance of supply chain management in the aerospace sector and its impact on Honeywell's future performance.

### Quarterly Dividend Announcement

On July 26, 2024, Honeywell announced a quarterly dividend payment of $1.08 per share on the company's common stock ([Stock Analysis](https://stockanalysis.com/stocks/hon/)). This dividend reflects the company's commitment to returning value to shareholders and its strong cash flow generation capabilities.

### Profit Guidance Revision

Honeywell recently lowered its full-year profit guidance, which led to a 4% decline in its stock price during intraday trading ([Stock Analysis](https://stockanalysis.com/stocks/hon/)). This revision was attributed to management's outlook on various operational challenges and market conditions. Despite this, the company's long-term growth prospects remain strong, as indicated by the positive analyst ratings and price targets.

### Chief Financial Officer Succession

On September 5, 2024, Honeywell announced that Mike Stepniak, currently Vice President and Chief Financial Officer for Honeywell Aerospace Technologies, would succeed Greg Lewis as Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer following the company's Q4 and full-year financial results for fiscal year 2024 ([Honeywell](https://www.honeywell.com/us/en/press/2024/09/honeywell-announces-chief-financial-officer-succession)). This transition is part of the company's ongoing succession planning and is not expected to impact its performance or outlook.

### Strategic Acquisitions

Honeywell has been active in strategic acquisitions to bolster its market position. Recently, the company completed the acquisition of Cobham Advanced Electronic Solutions Inc. from Advent International, L.P. ([Simply Wall St](https://simplywall.st/stocks/us/capital-goods/nasdaq-hon/honeywell-international)). This acquisition is expected to enhance Honeywell's capabilities in advanced electronic solutions and contribute to its long-term growth.

## Future Growth Prospects

### Earnings and Revenue Growth

Honeywell's earnings are forecasted to grow at a rate of 9.5% per year, while revenue is expected to grow at 6.1% per year ([Simply Wall St](https://simplywall.st/stocks/us/capital-goods/nasdaq-hon/honeywell-international/future)). ### Return on Equity
