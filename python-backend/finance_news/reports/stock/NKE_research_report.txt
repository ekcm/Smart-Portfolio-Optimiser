# Financial News Report on Nike Inc. (NKE) Stock as of September 16, 2024

## Introduction

Nike Inc. (NYSE: NKE), a global leader in the design, development, marketing, and sale of athletic footwear, apparel, equipment, and accessories, has been navigating a challenging landscape in 2024. This report provides a comprehensive overview of the financial performance, key events, and market sentiment surrounding Nike's stock as of September 16, 2024. The analysis is based on the latest available data and news reports.

## Financial Performance Overview

### Valuation and Market Metrics

As of September 16, 2024, Nike's market capitalization stands at $118.46 billion, with an enterprise value of $118.83 billion. The company's trailing price-to-earnings (P/E) ratio is 21.18, while the forward P/E ratio is 25.58. The price-to-sales (P/S) ratio is 2.35, and the price-to-book (P/B) ratio is 8.21. The enterprise value to revenue (EV/Revenue) ratio is 2.31, and the enterprise value to EBITDA (EV/EBITDA) ratio is 16.61 ([Yahoo Finance](https://sg.finance.yahoo.com/quote/NKE/)).

### Profitability and Income Statement

Nike's profitability metrics are robust, with a profit margin of 11.10%, a return on assets (ROA) of 11.16%, and a return on equity (ROE) of 40.09%. The company's revenue for the trailing twelve months (TTM) is $51.36 billion, and the net income attributable to common shareholders is $5.7 billion. The diluted earnings per share (EPS) for the TTM is $3.73 ([Yahoo Finance](https://sg.finance.yahoo.com/quote/NKE/)).

### Balance Sheet and Cash Flow

Nike's balance sheet shows total cash of $11.58 billion and a total debt-to-equity ratio of 83.04%. The company's levered free cash flow for the TTM is $5.9 billion ([Yahoo Finance](https://sg.finance.yahoo.com/quote/NKE/)).

## Recent Stock Performance

### Stock Price Movement

In the latest trading session, Nike's stock closed at $78.40, marking a 0.4% increase from the previous day. However, the stock has faced significant challenges throughout the year. Year-to-date (YTD), the stock is down 2.01%, and over the past year, it has declined by 15.66%. The three-year and five-year returns are -21.13% and -27.23%, respectively ([Yahoo Finance](https://uk.finance.yahoo.com/quote/NKE/news/)).

### Analyst Ratings and Forecasts

According to 36 analysts, the average rating for Nike's stock is "Buy," with a 12-month price forecast of $101.85, representing a potential upside of 28.91% from the current price ([Stock Analysis](https://stockanalysis.com/stocks/nke/)).

## Key Events and Developments

### Shareholder Votes and Proposals

Nike shareholders recently voted against a labor rights proposal that would have required the company to publish a report evaluating the impact of worker-led interventions and legally binding agreements on its due diligence process ([Yahoo Finance](https://uk.finance.yahoo.com/quote/NKE/news/)). This decision has sparked discussions about the company's commitment to social responsibility and labor practices.

### Strategic Shifts and Cost-Cutting Measures

Under the leadership of CEO John Donahoe, Nike has implemented several strategic shifts and cost-cutting measures. In June 2023, the company warned of a weak start to fiscal 2024 due to cautious consumer behavior in the U.S. In December 2023, Nike announced a $2 billion cost savings program, which included reducing the number of Nike products and cutting about 2% of its total workforce ([MarketScreener](https://in.marketscreener.com/business-leaders/JOHN-DONAHOE-2584/news/Key-events-at-Nike-under-CEO-John-Donahoe-47841145/)).

### Financial Results and Guidance

Nike reported its fiscal 2024 fourth quarter and full year results on June 27, 2024. Full-year revenues were $51.4 billion, up 1% on a currency-neutral basis. However, fourth-quarter revenues were $12.6 billion, down 2% on a reported basis and flat on a currency-neutral basis. Nike Direct revenues for the fourth quarter were $5.1 billion, down 8% on a reported basis and down 7% on a currency-neutral basis. Wholesale revenues for the fourth quarter were $7.1 billion, up 5% on a reported basis and up 8% on a currency-neutral basis. The gross margin for the fourth quarter increased by 110 basis points to 44.7%, and diluted EPS was $0.99 ([Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/nike-inc-reports-fiscal-2024-201500243.html)).

### Investor Sentiment and Market Reactions

Investor sentiment towards Nike has been mixed. Billionaire investor William Ackman built new stakes in Nike, signaling confidence in the company's long-term prospects. However, the stock has faced pressure due to weak sales forecasts and concerns about the effectiveness of its direct-to-consumer strategy ([MarketScreener](https://in.marketscreener.com/business-leaders/JOHN-DONAHOE-2584/news/Key-events-at-Nike-under-CEO-John-Donahoe-47841145/)).

### Competitive Landscape

Nike's stock performance has lagged behind its competitors. Shares of rivals Adidas, Hoka owner Deckers, and On Holding have risen by 16%, 33%, and 65%, respectively, in the same period. This disparity highlights the challenges Nike faces in maintaining its market leadership amid increasing competition ([MarketScreener](https://in.marketscreener.com/business-leaders/JOHN-DONAHOE-2584/news/Key-events-at-Nike-under-CEO-John-Donahoe-47841145/)).

## Conclusion

Nike Inc. is navigating a complex and challenging environment in 2024. The company's financial performance shows resilience, with strong profitability metrics and a solid balance sheet. However, the stock has faced significant headwinds, including weak sales forecasts, strategic shifts, and competitive pressures. The recent shareholder vote against the labor rights proposal and the mixed investor sentiment further underscore the challenges Nike faces.

Despite these challenges, Nike's long-term prospects remain promising, as evidenced by the positive analyst ratings and the strategic investments by prominent investors like William Ackman. The company's focus on innovation, digital transformation, and cost-cutting measures could position it for a stronger performance in the future.

As Nike continues to navigate these challenges, investors will closely monitor its strategic initiatives, financial performance, and market dynamics to assess its potential for long-term growth and value creation.
