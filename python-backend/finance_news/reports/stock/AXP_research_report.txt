# Financial Report on American Express (AXP) Stock as of September 16, 2024

## Introduction

American Express Company (NYSE: AXP) is a globally recognized financial services corporation, known for its charge and credit card products, as well as travel-related services. This report provides a comprehensive analysis of the financial performance and key events surrounding AXP stock as of September 16, 2024. The analysis is based on recent earnings reports, stock performance, and strategic initiatives undertaken by the company.

## Earnings Performance

### Fiscal Year 2024 Earnings Growth

American Express (AXP) has demonstrated robust earnings growth for the fiscal year 2024. The company's earnings are expected to grow by 17% for the current fiscal year ([Zacks](https://www.zacks.com/stock/news/2333912/earnings-growth-price-strength-make-american-express-axp-a-stock-to-watch)). This growth is supported by several factors, including increased consumer spending, strategic partnerships, and expansion into new markets.

### Quarterly Earnings Reports

1. **Q1 2024**: The earnings call for the period ending March 31, 2024, highlighted strong financial performance, driven by increased card member spending and higher loan balances ([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/quote/nyse/axp/)).
2. **Q4 2023**: The earnings call for the period ending December 31, 2023, showed a significant increase in revenue and net income, attributed to the holiday season and strategic marketing campaigns ([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/quote/nyse/axp/)).
3. **Q3 2023**: The earnings call for the period ending September 30, 2023, indicated steady growth in card member spending and a strong performance in the travel and entertainment sectors ([The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/quote/nyse/axp/)).

### Analyst Revisions and Consensus Estimates

Seven analysts have revised their earnings estimates upwards in the last 60 days for fiscal 2024. The Zacks Consensus Estimate has increased by $0.14 to $13.12 per share. AXP boasts an average earnings surprise of 7.7% ([Zacks](https://www.zacks.com/stock/news/2333912/earnings-growth-price-strength-make-american-express-axp-a-stock-to-watch)).

## Stock Performance

### Market Capitalization and Valuation

As of September 13, 2024, AXP has a market capitalization of $184.13 billion. The stock's trailing P/E ratio is 19.33, and its forward P/E ratio is 17.42. The PEG ratio (5-year expected) stands at 1.96, indicating a balanced growth relative to its earnings ([Yahoo Finance](https://sg.finance.yahoo.com/quote/AXP/)).

### Price Performance

Since being added to the Zacks Focus List on December 23, 2021, at $162.47 per share, AXP shares have increased by 54.44% to $250.92 ([Zacks](https://www.zacks.com/stock/news/2333912/earnings-growth-price-strength-make-american-express-axp-a-stock-to-watch)). This significant appreciation reflects the company's strong financial performance and investor confidence.

### Dividend Announcements

American Express has declared a quarterly dividend on its 3.550% Fixed Rate Reset Noncumulative Preferred Shares, Series D, of $9,269.44 per share, payable on June 17, 2024, to shareholders of record on June 1, 2024 ([Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/quote/AXP/press-releases/)).

## Strategic Initiatives and Partnerships

### Travel and Lifestyle Services

American Express continues to enhance its travel and lifestyle services, offering exclusive experiences and benefits to its card members. The company has introduced the Premium Events Collection™, providing curated experiences in sports, fashion, dining, and entertainment ([American Express](https://www.americanexpress.com/en-us/benefits/membership/experiences/)).

### Investor Conferences

American Express executives are actively participating in investor conferences to discuss the company's strategic direction and financial performance. On September 4, 2024, Anna Marrs, Group President, Global Commercial Services and Credit & Fraud Risk, participated in a fireside chat at an investor conference ([American Express](https://ir.americanexpress.com/news/investor-relations-news/investor-relations-news-details/2024/American-Express-Executives-to-Participate-in-Upcoming-Investor-Conferences/default.aspx)).

### Strategic Partnerships

American Express has partnered with Delta Air Lines® to reintroduce the popular airplane metal card design, enhancing its product offerings and customer engagement ([Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/quote/AXP/press-releases/)).
